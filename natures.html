Natures

All Pokemon have one of the 25 natures shown in the table to the right. All natures affects the value of two of the pokemons stats, more specifically it raises one stat by 10% and decreases one stat by 10%. All base stats can be affected except hp, which is never affected.

With 5 remaining stats (Atk, Sp. Atk, Def, Sp. Def & Speed) which each can be increased and/or decreased, this leaves a total of 25 possible natures. The 5 natures which increase and decrease the same stat are called neutral natures, and has no impact on the pokemons stats.
